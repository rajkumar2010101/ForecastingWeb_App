This Flask web application enables users to upload Workforce Management (WFM) datasets containing weekly call volume data. It preprocesses the data, extracts trends, and applies forecasting models to predict call volumes for the upcoming week.

How It Works

1. Data Upload & Preprocessing

Users upload a CSV or Excel file containing columns such as Year, Week, Mon, Tue, Wed, Thu, Fri, and Total Offered.

2. Trend Identification & Factors Considered

  • Time-Based Patterns: Analyzes weekly call volumes over time to identify trends.
  • Seasonality: Detects recurring patterns, such as higher call traffic on specific weekdays.
  • Variability & Fluctuations: Models like ARIMA and Holt-Winters capture changes in data while smoothing irregularities.

3. Forecasting Process

The selected model (ARIMA, Holt-Winters, or Random Forecasting) predicts call volumes for Monday to Friday of the next week. The application calculates Mean Absolute Percentage Error (MAPE) to evaluate model accuracy.
